White Water Rafting

Purpose

Our purpose is to create safe, exciting, and memorable rafting adventures that help families and friends connect with nature and with each other...

History

White Water Rafting began as a small family-run adventure group over twenty years ago, when a passionate guide and his friends started offering weekend trips along a local river. It started as a handful of rafts and a love for the outdoors.

Today we continue that spirit of exploration by welcoming guests from around the world. Our history is built on respect for the river, safety on every trip and creating memories that last long after the water has calmed.
