White Water Rafting

Purpose

Our purpose is to create safe, exciting, and memorable rafting adventures that help families and friends connect with nature and with each other...

History

White Water Rafting Co. began as a small family-run adventure group over twenty years ago, when a passionate guide and his friends started offering weekend trips along a local river. What started as a handful of rafts and a love for the outdoors quickly grew as word spread about their safe, exciting, and friendly experiences. Through dedication, training, and a deep respect for nature, the company expanded into one of the most trusted rafting outfitters in the region, welcoming thousands of adventurers every year.
